SELF PORTAIT

Origin,jouney and consequence This work is a photo montage created with the software Adobe Photoshop. The objective of this project was to create a composition in which I could describe me as a person, in order to do that I used different symbolic elements and distributed the objects and colors to create a composition that gave details about my personality. To perform this project I based on three artistic movements, those are, surrealism, pop art and the Renaissance, since I believed that those were the ones that best fit with the message I wanted to convey. Prohibido

Prohibido Is a university work designed for a hypothetic music album, in this project I had to create the Album cover in which I tried to capture the sounds and meaning of the songs, the essence of the musical style is tried to be achieved through symbology, in which the heart symbolizes the forbidden fruit and the snake the temptation or the search for truth. In terms of composition, I used chromatic colors and a more symmetrical and centered structure.

I did also an AR filter for instagram used to promote the album, in which you can see a heart beating and in its surrounding circles that are spinning over it, also you can hear the sound of the beats and some music in the background. The 3d animation appears when your phone detects the image of the cover.

To create this filter I used 4 programs, this are photoshop, Zbrush, 3ds max and Spark AR. The first thing that I did was downloading a 3d figure of the heart, I searched for a 3d model similar to the heart in the cover, then I imported the file into zbrush where I did some changes to the topology and created the texture by polypaint, to create it I based on the picture of the original heart in the cover. After that I imported the file into 3ds max where I created the animation in order to see the movement of the beats and finally, I imported everything into spark AR to create the filter.

ILUSTRATION

PORTRAIT 1 This is a digital illustration developed in Clip Studio software. The creative process was very interesting because it was a personal project, so I had total freedom to experiment and learn during the process. The first thing I did was looking for a reference image, when I had defined the shape of the character, I began to create a color palette for the skin, eyes and hair, after this I began to apply the different tones to the character, always respecting the lights and the shadows, finally for the background I chose some pastel or earthy colors and applied them on the background, then I mixed them creating a very interesting visual effect.

Cadira EPS

  • Cadira EPS was a university group project, it consisted in creating the brand image of a hypothetical company and the elaboration of its main product, a chair.

  • To start the project first we developed a creative Briefing, in order to identify the needs of the company and to know in better way what kind of product they wanted.

  • After having this clear, we began to develop the brand image, creating different sketches of the logo and choosing the colours that best fits with the brand values.

  • In the creative process of the chair, we first proposed different designs, until finally we chose a final prototype, which we believed to be the most ergonomic, easy to build and economical. We made a model of the chair in foam board, a 3D render and finally, we create the real prototype with wood.

UX & UI

New Portal Rest This project consisted of redesigning and restructuring a food application called Portal Rest. To carry out the final product we had to do different studies to analyse and find out what was not working well into the app, we did a heuristic, a guerrilla test and a tree testing. Once we have done this studies, we made different sketches for the final prototype and through the Adobe XD program we created the high-fidelity prototype, giving the app a new usability and making it more usable.

environment design

  • Oasis Is a natural environment created with the program "3D Unreal Engine". To create this environment, I downloaded different elements from the Megascans gallery such as plants, stones and textures, then I modeled the cave in "Zbrush" and placed these elements so that look as natural as possible, finally I put lights on the scene to create a greater effect of realism.

  • For this project I was inspired by the cenotes and oases that are places where there can be vegetation in an arid environment due to the presence of water, within the scene this effect can be achieved through the use of arid or desert textures that contrast with the plants and water.

Poster

  • Art Nouveau Poster This is an illustration that I developed with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Clip Studio, it was a complementary work for the Cadira EPS project, which consisted of creating an Advertising Poster to promote a chair, this poster had to follow an aesthetic and preserve the characteristics of Art Nouveau.

  • To create this illustration, I based on different artists of the movement, mainly in Alfons Mucha, who had a much more naturalistic and ornamental approach. The adobe illustrator software helped me a lot to create all the ornaments of the illustration, since, due to its vector drawing, it simplifies the creation of symmetrical and repetitive forms. For the character`s elaboration I used clip studio since it is a very good software to make digital drawings, finally I used Photoshop to give color and texture to all the background ornament.

  • The objective of the poster is to give a feeling of warmth and freshness, for this reason I used warm colors that evoke nature, such as red, green, brown and yellow.
